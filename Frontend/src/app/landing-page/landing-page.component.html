Features

Features marked with Pro are accessible to logged in users

Search for your food, conveniently!

With foodzer you can just search for the food, and get instant results based on any brand you like. Our catalog contains millions of food items for you to choose upon and view. You can filter the food items based on your brands, or get an non-branded food as well. There is always something for someone!

Create the meal that suits you!

With foodzer's intuitive interface you can easily add your foods from search to your meals, and create the perfect meal of your choice, or your mood or both. We also have the feature to get the total nutritional value in case you are health conscious!

Save foods you love for later!

Crazy about a food that you got from our catalog, or you are frequently using some food items regualarly to the meal! Wont it be great if you could just save them! We have got you covered. You can save the foods you absolutely love to your favourites and access them easily whenever you want. Great! isn't it?

Recommendations are great! isn't it?

Using our beloved application for the first time? Quite confused which foods you wanna choose for you next great meal. Why not look at our recommendation section? It has catalog of great foods recommended by users just like you. And moreover it is community driven and have been added by users just like you! So give it a try!
